About Us

Huckleberry's signature blend is composed of 70% Arabica coffee that is imported from Brazil and 30% Robusta coffee from Sumatra. This combination gives you coffee that is very flavorful and aromatic with chocolaty and fruity notes. Many are also hooked with their best-selling drink, the Vietnamese Latte. It is made from Nguyen beans from Vietnam. These beans have rich and strong flavor that blends beautifully with sweetened milk giving you a rich and flavorful coffee with hints of chocolaty tones.
